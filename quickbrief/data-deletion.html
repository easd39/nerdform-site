QuickBrief - Data Deletion Instructions

At QuickBrief, we respect your right to control your personal data. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time using one of the methods below.

How to Delete Your Data

Method 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

  1. Open the QuickBrief app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Scroll down to Account
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm your request

Your account and all data will be deleted immediately.

Method 2: Email Request

Send an email to contact@nerdform.org with:

  • Subject: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Email address associated with your QuickBrief account
  • Reason (optional)

We will process your request within 30 days.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Data Type Deletion Timeline
Account Information (email, name) Immediate (or within 30 days if requested via email)
User-Submitted Content (texts, images) Immediate (or within 30 days if requested via email)
Generated Summaries Immediate (or within 30 days if requested via email)
Usage Data (analytics) Immediate (or within 30 days if requested via email)
Subscription Information Immediate (managed by RevenueCat and Apple)
⚠️ Important Notes:
  • Account deletion is permanent and irreversible
  • All your summaries, settings, and preferences will be lost
  • Active subscriptions will be canceled (no refunds for unused time)
  • Some data may be retained for legal compliance (e.g., tax records) but will not be used for any other purpose

Data Retention After Deletion

After you delete your account:

  • Personal data is immediately removed from active systems
  • Backup copies are purged within 90 days
  • Some anonymized usage statistics may be retained for service improvement
  • Legal/financial records are retained as required by law (minimum 7 years for tax purposes)
