Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-02

This Privacy Policy explains how NerdForm Labs LLC (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information in connection with the MuscleNerd mobile application and related support pages.

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate and improve the app:

  • Diagnostics (e.g., crash logs, performance data) to maintain reliability.
  • Basic analytics (e.g., anonymized usage events) to understand feature usage.
  • Support communications (your email and message) when you contact us voluntarily.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not collect precise location, contacts, or other sensitive categories unless explicitly provided by you for support.

How We Use Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve app functionality and stability.
  • To respond to support requests and communicate with you about issues you report.
  • To analyze anonymized trends and enhance user experience.

Third-Party Services

We may use privacy-respecting analytics and crash reporting services (e.g., platform-standard SDKs). These providers process limited technical data on our behalf to deliver their services. Where applicable, their processing is governed by their own privacy terms.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for the purposes described above, or as required by law. Support emails are kept for troubleshooting and audit history, then deleted or anonymized.

Children’s Privacy

MuscleNerd is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can delete it.

Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices & Rights

You can choose not to send support emails or optional data. Where applicable by law, you may request access, correction, or deletion of your information by contacting us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page and update the “Last updated” date above.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact us at contact@nerdform.org.
