QuickBrief — Data Deletion Instructions

At QuickBrief, we respect your right to control your personal data. This page explains how your data is managed and how you can request its deletion at any time.

ℹ️ Automatic Deletion: Most of your data is deleted automatically. Study sessions — including summaries, flashcards, and any submitted text or images — are stored for 7 days and then permanently deleted. You can also extend individual sessions by 7 days or delete them manually at any time from the app.

How to Delete Your Account & All Data

Method 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

  1. Open the QuickBrief app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your request

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted immediately.

Method 2: Email Request

Send an email to contact@nerdform.org with:

  • Subject: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Email address associated with your QuickBrief account
  • Reason (optional)

We will process your request within 30 days.

What Data Is Collected & Deleted

Data Type Deletion Timeline
Account information (email, sign-in provider) Immediately upon account deletion
Submitted text content Auto-deleted after 7 days, or immediately upon account deletion
Submitted images (used for text recognition) Auto-deleted after 7 days, or immediately upon account deletion
Generated summaries & flashcards Auto-deleted after 7 days, or immediately upon account deletion
Usage statistics (scan & grade counts) Immediately upon account deletion
Subscription information Managed by RevenueCat, Apple, and Google Play — subject to their policies
⚠️ Important Notes:
  • Account deletion is permanent and irreversible
  • All study sessions, flashcards, and history will be permanently lost
  • Deleting your account does not automatically cancel an active subscription — manage subscriptions through the App Store or Google Play
  • Some anonymized, non-identifiable usage data may be retained for service improvement
  • Legal and financial records are retained as required by applicable law

Managing Individual Study Sessions

You can delete or manage individual study sessions without deleting your account:

  • Open the History tab in the app
  • Swipe left on any session to delete it immediately
  • Tap +7 days to extend a session's retention period
  • All sessions expire and are deleted automatically after 7 days
